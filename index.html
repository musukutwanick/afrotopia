Our Story

Welcome to Afrotopia Café

Located at Theatre in the Park Harare Gardens in Harare, Afrotopia Café is more than just a restaurant – it’s a celebration of Zimbabwean art, culture and cuisine.

Our café was born from a passion to create a space where visitors can immerse themselves in the rich cultural heritage of Zimbabwe while enjoying delicious, locally-inspired dishes prepared with care and creativity.

At Afrotopia, we believe that food is an art form. Our chefs craft each dish as a culinary masterpiece, combining the best flavors with modern cooking techniques to create an unforgettable dining experience.
